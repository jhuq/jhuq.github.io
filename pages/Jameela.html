InTune & Premium Pitch

InTune & Premium Pitch

Jameela is now a Software Engineer at LinkedIn, San Francisco California, where she is also music director for LinkedIn acapella group called Internote. This group annually competes in Techapella. Techapella is an organization of acapella groups from all major bay area tech companies. Due to quarantine restriction in 2020, it was a virtual performance.

This YouTube video is LinkedIn, InTune and, Premium Pitch, performing the song You Won't Let Go by James Arthur.

Iowa-Agni

Iowa Agni at Gathe Raho

Jameela at the University of Iowa while doing her masters’ in physics and chemistry took a role as director of UOI musical group Iowa Agni. Iowa Agni is an acapella musical group that plays blended music based on Indian music and English Pop Tunes; while she was director of Agni they hosted Gathe Raho. Gathe Raho is one of the biggest academic Bollywood music competitions with similar acapella teams in the US. These groups from all of the North American colleges get invited.

The song Iowa Agni sang during the event Stronger/Challa was arranged by Jameela in both English and Urdu.
